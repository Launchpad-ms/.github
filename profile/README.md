### Launching Your Product

1.  **Create an Account** - Sign up at `https://launchpad.ms/sign-up`
2.  **Submit Your Product** - Navigate to the "Launch" page
3.  **Fill Product Details** - Include name, description, category, and media
4.  **Choose Launch Type**:

*   **Standard Launch** - Free submission with organic visibility
*   **Premium Launch** - Featured placement, guaranteed backlinks, priority scheduling
